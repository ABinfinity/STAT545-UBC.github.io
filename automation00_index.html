Tab gotcha

Make is rather picky and requires that lines be indented with tabs and not spaces. In the test drive we explained how to globally ask RStudio to NOT replace tabs with spaces. Below in the hands-on activity, we explain how to incorporate this request into the settings for a specific RStudio Project.

Please pause here and take a moment for this to truly sink in. When writing a Makefile, it is very important to indent with tabs.

If you mess up, here’s the type of error you might see:

Error: `makefile:2: *** missing separator. Stop.`
